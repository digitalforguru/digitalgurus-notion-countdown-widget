FAQ

How do I add this to Notion?

Copy your embed link, go to Notion, type /embed, paste the link, and confirm!

Can I change the countdown later?

Yes! Come back to this builder page, update your dates, label, icon, or style, then copy the new link.

What can I count down to?

Anything with a date: trips, birthdays, holidays, launches, deadlines, school events, or personal goals.
